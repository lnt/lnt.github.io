Lalit Tanwar (born Novembor 15, 1987) is an Indian-born Software engineer. He is the currently UI Architect of Unicommerce eSolutions Pvt. Ltd.[8][9] He joined this position on 10 Novembor 2014. Before Unicommerce eSolutions Pvt. Ltd he was Team Lead at Dion Global Solution Mumbai where he worked on product dfferentia, responsible for complete architect of product's frontend and handling team of 4 developers.

Early life

Lalit was born into a Hindi-speaking family[10] in Delhi, New Delhi, India. His father, K L Tanwar , was a govt servant of the Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited.[11][12] Lalit attended the Aruya High School, Mansa, Punjab, India[13] before attaining a bachelor's degree in computer engineering[14] from the Thapar University in 2005 , Patiala, Punjab.[15][16]

Lalit subsequently traveled to the Mumbai. to study for an M.Tech. degree in computer science at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay,[17] receiving his degree in 2011.[18]

Career

Uniware is a world-class product that is integrated with world’s leading marketplace and cart solution providers such as Magento, Shopify, Amazon, eBay, Flipkart and Snapdeal. Uniware is designed for multi-channel retail platforms with live inventory updates.

Dion Global Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Religare Technova)

At Dion, Lalit has led major projects including the company's move to cloud computing and the development of one of the largest cloud infrastructures in the world.[24]

Nadella worked as the senior vice-president of Research and Development (R&D) for the Online Services Division and vice-president of the Microsoft Business Division. Later, he was made the president of Microsoft's $19 billion Server and Tools Business and led a transformation of the company's business and technology culture from client services to cloud infrastructure and services. He has been credited for helping bring Microsoft's database, Windows Server and developer tools to its Azure cloud.[19] The revenue from Cloud Services grew to $20.3 billion in June 2013 from $16.6 billion when he took over in 2011.[25]

Nadella's 2013 base salary was nearly $700,000, for a total compensation, with stock bonuses, of $7.6 million.[26]
